FAQ's


What services do you offer?
We offer a range of services including event hosting, emceeing, singing performances, and entertainment for various occasions.
How can I book Aryan Minocha for an event?
Booking is easy! Simply visit the "Contact" page on my website or send an email to contact@aryanminocha.com with your event details and requirements.
Where are your upcoming performances scheduled?
You can find the schedule of my upcoming performances under the "Events" section of the website. It's regularly updated.
What sets Aryan Minocha apart as an entertainer?
With my unique blend of charisma, talent, and experience, I create unforgettable moments that elevate your event to a new level of entertainment.
Can I request a specific song for a performance?
Yes, I'm open to song requests. Feel free to suggest your favorite song, and I'll do my best to include it in my performance.
Are you available for international events?
Yes, I'm available for international bookings. Contact me for details and availability.
What is the process for collaborating on projects?
To collaborate on projects, simply reach out via the "Contact" page or email. We'll discuss your project's details, goals, and requirements.
Do you offer singing lessons or workshops?
Currently, I'm focused on performances and events. While I don't offer lessons, you can enjoy my singing at live shows and events.
How can I stay updated on your latest news and events?
Stay connected by following me on social media (links on the website) and subscribing to my newsletter for updates on news, events, and more.
Do you provide customized event hosting solutions?
Absolutely! I tailor my hosting style to match the theme and vibe of your event, ensuring a personalized and engaging experience.
